DIY LED Tape

At Renaissance, I was tasked with a stage redesign for the Easter Services. I looked at the available resources and came up with the idea of LED tape bars just in front of the upstage curtain. I used DMX decoders that we already had use in the past in the old sanctuary, spare LED tape metal gutters, and wood to construct the fixtures. I then used 12 gague thermostat wire to run data to the fixtures. This created a very visually interesting look for the stage that had been in use since installation.
